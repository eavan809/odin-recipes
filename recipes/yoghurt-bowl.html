Yoghurt Bowl

Home Page

Description

This is a template for a basic yoghurt bowl. Perfect for a versatile and nutritious breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Yoghurt of choice - Flavour of choice - Large tub
  • Granola of choice
  • Fruit or fruits of choice
  • Chia seeds

Steps

  1. Fill the bowl with desired amount of yoghurt
  2. Pour granola over half of the bowl
  3. Chop fruit as desired and place on top of yoghurt
  4. Pour over chia seeds
  5. Mix if desired and enjoy
