Quick falafels

Home Page

Description

This isn't much of a recipe rather just a mix of foods I enjoy together. It uses falafel balls from any shop and a mix of other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Any falafel balls to cook from a shop
  • Coleslaw
  • Eggs
  • Chilli crisp
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Orange juice

Steps

  1. Put the falafel balls in the airfryer for 10 minutes at 180 oC - As many as desired
  2. Wait about five minutes
  3. Cook the eggs
  4. If frying put hot water on the pan and crack eggs in then fry for a minute before putting on a cover and frying for another two minutes and season with salt and pepper
  5. If scrambling heat butter in a pot and crack eggs in then whisk around with a fork for a few minutes and add a little bit of milk and season with salt and pepper
  6. Plate falafels and eggs
  7. Add coleslaw to the plate
  8. Pour chilli crisp over the eggs
  9. Pour a glass of orange juice
  10. Enjoy
